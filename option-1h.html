Straight answers
What hospital leaders actually ask us
The four objections we hear most — and how the partnership answers them.
Talk it through with us
"Why not just recruit a pain physician and do this ourselves?"⌄
Recruiting one physician isn't a program. We bring the clinical model, the operational playbook, and the bench strength to make a service line last — especially in small communities.
"We don't want to share revenue — is the JV really worth it?"⌄
In our joint‑venture model, PMG only succeeds when the program succeeds — so incentives are truly aligned. We'll walk through the economics, the path to profitability, and how startup investment is shared, in plain numbers.
"Are we giving up operating control?"⌄
No. Programs run inside your hospital, under your brand, with governance you help define — and full transparency into operations, compliance, and results. We speak your language on Stark, documentation, and billing.
"Will the program actually last in a community our size?"⌄
20 years, 40 partnerships, 95% retention. Longevity is the metric we're proudest of — and the one rural communities need most. Board‑certified, fellowship‑trained physicians and a rigorous APP onboarding process are how we make it stick.
