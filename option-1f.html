Talk It Through With Us
"Why not just do this ourselves?"⌄
Recruiting one physician isn't a program. We bring the clinical model, the operational playbook, and the bench strength to make a service line last — especially in small communities where a single departure can end everything.
"Are incentives really aligned?"⌄
In our joint-venture model, PMG only succeeds when the program succeeds. What's good for the patient is good for the hospital, the providers, and the community — by design, not by promise.
"Do we give up operating control?"⌄
No. Programs run inside your hospital, under your brand, with governance you help define — and full transparency into operations, compliance, and results.
"Will the program actually last?"⌄
20 years, 40 partnerships, 95% retention. Longevity is the metric we're proudest of — and the one rural communities need most.
